# Powerbi-Retail-Sales-Analysis-dashboard-
Retail Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail coffee shop sales data to identify sales trends, customer demand patterns, store performance, and product category contributions. The dashboard was developed using Power BI to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

The analysis helps management understand:

- Overall sales performance
- Peak and off-peak business periods
- Store-wise revenue contribution
- Product category performance
- Customer demand behavior across time

---

🎯 Business Problem

Retail coffee businesses often rely on assumptions rather than data when making operational decisions. Without proper analytics, organizations may face:

- Overstaffing during low-demand periods
- Understaffing during peak hours
- Inefficient inventory planning
- Reduced operational efficiency

This project aims to provide a data-driven solution through interactive dashboards and visual analytics.

---

🎯 Objectives

Primary Objectives

- Analyze overall sales trends
- Identify busiest and slowest days
- Determine peak transaction hours

Secondary Objectives

- Compare performance across store locations
- Analyze product category contribution
- Support staffing and operational planning
- Improve decision-making through data visualization

---

📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains transaction-level retail sales records.

Columns Used

Column Name| Description
transaction_id| Unique transaction identifier
transaction_date| Transaction date
transaction_time| Transaction time
transaction_qty| Quantity sold
unit_price| Price per unit
store_id| Store identifier
store_location| Store location
product_id| Product identifier
product_category| Product category
product_type| Product type
product_detail| Product details

---

🧹 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

The following data preparation steps were performed:

- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Validated data types
- Created calculated revenue field
- Extracted hour and day attributes
- Created time buckets (Morning, Afternoon, Evening)

Revenue Calculation

Revenue = Transaction Quantity × Unit Price

- Evaluated revenue contribution by category

Store-wise Analysis

- Compared revenue across store locations
- Identified top-performing stores

Time-based Analysis

- Analyzed hourly transaction patterns
- Identified peak demand periods

---

🔍 Key Insights

- Coffee products generated the highest revenue contribution.
- Revenue remained relatively stable across the analysis period.
- Certain hours experienced significantly higher transaction volume.
- Store performance was consistent across locations.
- Customer demand varied by time of day and day of week.

💡 Recommendations

Operational Improvements

- Increase staffing during peak demand hours.
- Optimize workforce allocation during low-demand periods.
- Improve inventory planning based on demand trends.

Business Strategy

- Expand high-performing product categories.
- Launch targeted promotions during slow periods.
- Continuously monitor performance using dashboard insights.
